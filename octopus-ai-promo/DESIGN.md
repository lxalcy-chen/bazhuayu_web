# 八爪鱼 AI Agent Promo Design

## Style Prompt
Premium white-and-blue Chinese SaaS technology video for ecommerce business owners. The look should feel bright, clean, confident, and high-value: glassmorphism panels, soft blue shadows, crisp data cards, orbiting platform logos, and subtle 3D depth. The video should look like a polished product launch reel, not a generic corporate slideshow.

## Colors
- Canvas: `#f7fbff`
- Ink: `#07111f`
- Primary Blue: `#1b70ff`
- Deep Blue: `#0b3ea8`
- Cyan Accent: `#39d6ff`
- Muted Text: `#5b6c82`
- Glass White: `rgba(255,255,255,0.78)`

## Typography
- Primary Chinese/UI font: `"Microsoft YaHei", "PingFang SC", Arial, sans-serif`
- Use heavy display weights for hero lines and clear medium weights for captions.
- Numbers use tabular lining where possible.

## Motion
- Smooth premium SaaS motion: soft upward entrances, orbiting platform nodes, line drawing, glass cards sliding in.
- Use scene transitions with blue-white light wipes or soft crossfades.
- Keep motion confident and readable; avoid chaotic or overly fast animation.

## What NOT To Do
- Do not use dark cyberpunk backgrounds.
- Do not use purple blobs, beige palettes, or generic stock photo styling.
- Do not use tiny body text that will be unreadable in a rendered video.
- Do not show internal strategy wording.
- Do not overload scenes with too many labels at once.
